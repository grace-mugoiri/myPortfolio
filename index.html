About Me

I'm Grace Mugoiri, a passionate Software Developer who loves solving real-world problems through creative digital solutions. I have experience in web development, backend systems, and blockchain technology.

I'm dedicated to continuous learning, building beautiful user experiences, and contributing to open-source projects.
An active member of Bitcoin DADA and Tech Sisters Kenya , where I collaborate with like-minded individuals to empower women in tech and blockchain spaces.

My journey into development is driven by a deep curiosity and a desire to solve problems through code. I enjoy contributing to open-source projects, mentoring aspiring developers, and continuously learning new technologies. I have built projects like a Bitcoin wallet application using Flask and Bitcoin Core RPC, and I am eager to explore Lightning Network integration for faster payments.

I also happen to write tech articles which are all found on medium.
